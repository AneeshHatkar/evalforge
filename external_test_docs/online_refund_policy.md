# Online Store Refund Policy

## Return Window

Customers may request a return within 30 days of receiving the item.

If more than 30 days have passed since delivery, the customer is not eligible for a refund or exchange.

## Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return, the item must be unused, in the same condition it was received, and in the original packaging.

Items that are used, damaged by the customer, or missing original packaging may be rejected.

## Proof of Purchase

Customers must provide proof of purchase before a refund can be processed.

If proof of purchase is missing, the support agent should ask the customer to provide it before making a refund decision.

## Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted for items that are returned with minor issues, missing packaging, or signs of use.

## Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is caused by a seller error.

## Escalation

Suspicious refund requests, repeated refund attempts, or unclear return conditions should be escalated to human review.
