# Shipping Policy

## Standard Shipping

Standard shipping usually takes 5 to 7 business days after the order is processed.

## Delayed Delivery

A delivery is considered late if it arrives more than 7 days after the promised delivery date.

If the delivery is delayed but still within 7 days of the promised delivery date, the customer should be informed that the order is still within the normal delay window.

## Lost Packages

If tracking shows no movement for more than 10 days, the support agent should escalate the case for lost-package investigation.
