# Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty

Most products include a one-year limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects.

The warranty does not cover accidental damage, misuse, cosmetic wear, or unauthorized repairs.

## Warranty Claim Requirements

To process a warranty claim, the customer must provide the order ID, purchase date, product serial number, and a description of the issue.

If required information is missing, the support agent should ask for clarification before starting the warranty claim.

## Escalation

Warranty claims involving safety concerns, repeated failures, or unclear product damage should be escalated to human review.
