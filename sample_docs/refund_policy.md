# Refund Policy

## Late Delivery Refunds

Customers are eligible for a shipping-fee refund if delivery is delayed by more than 7 days and the customer has not already received compensation.

Customers are not eligible for a full product refund only because the package arrived late.

## Damaged Product Refunds

Customers may request a replacement or refund if the product arrives damaged and they provide photo evidence within 14 days of delivery.

If photo evidence is missing, the support agent should ask the customer to upload evidence before approving a refund.

## Duplicate Refund Requests

Customers who have already received compensation for the same order are not eligible for another refund.

Repeated refund requests for the same issue should be escalated to human review.
